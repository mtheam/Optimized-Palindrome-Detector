What are the longest palindromes?

In English, palindromic words can be 11 letters long such as DETARTRATED or AIBOHPHOBIA (neologism), there is also ROTAVATOR, REDIVIDER, MALAYALAM, EVITATIVE, DELEVELED, CINEGENIC in 9 letters.

In French, George Perec has written Le Grand Palindrome that counts up to 1247 words (text that can be read backwards/in reverse order).
